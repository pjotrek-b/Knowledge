#+ Job statuses

STATUS_STARTING: The job was dispatched. STATUS_STOPPING: The job is currently stopping. STATUS_IN_PROGRESS: The job was sent and is in progress. STATUS_COMPLETED: The job was performed and is successfully completed. STATUS_STOPPED: The job was stopped and most likely incomplete. STATUS_ERROR: There was an unrecoverable error during the job.

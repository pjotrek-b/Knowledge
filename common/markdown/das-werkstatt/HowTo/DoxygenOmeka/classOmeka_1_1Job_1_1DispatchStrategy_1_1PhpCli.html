Omeka\Job\DispatchStrategy\PhpCli::send ( Job  $job)

Perform the job in the background.

Jobs may need access to variables that are impossible to derive from outside a web context. Here we pass the variables via shell arguments. The perform-job script then sets them to the PHP-CLI context.
